FAQ

+

How much will it cost to install a fire alarm or air-conditioning unit?

The cost depends on several factors such as the building size, system type, materials used, and project requirements. To get an accurate quotation, you can contact us for a free assessment. Our team will evaluate your site and provide a detailed and transparent estimate.

+

How long does the installation process take?

Project duration varies depending on the scope of work. A typical fire alarm or air-conditioning installation may take a few days to several weeks. We always ensure that our projects are completed on schedule without compromising quality and safety standards.

+

Do you offer maintenance services after installation?

Yes, we provide maintenance and system check-up services to ensure your fire alarm and air-conditioning systems operate efficiently and safely. Regular maintenance helps extend the life of your equipment and prevent costly repairs.

+

Are your systems compliant with safety standards?

Absolutely. All our fire protection and air-conditioning installations follow Philippine safety codes and industry standards. Our professional team ensures that every project meets the required certifications and local regulations.

+

How can I request a site visit or quotation?

You can reach us through our email at samm1856sae@yahoo.com or give us a call to schedule a visit. Our team will assess your site and provide a customized plan and quotation based on your specific needs.
